Noise & The Novice

Presentation on 20 September 2018

Thus far, my program of study has been centered around the relationship between music and musical training.

  • What makes something music as opposed to noise?
  • Once we agree it's music, what makes it good music?
  • Once we're talking good music, what's the role of musical study and training in a person's ability to make good music?

This framing allowed me to ask and investigate lots of questions
I really want to ask.
